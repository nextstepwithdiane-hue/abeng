About Us

Comfort, safety, and peace of mind

San Jacinto Assisted Living, LLC is dedicated to enriching the lives of our residents through thoughtful care, engaging activities, and a true sense of belonging. Our dedicated team is here every step of the way to ensure comfort, safety, and peace of mind for residents and their families alike.

"Welcome to your new home." — our residents enjoy a warm, personalized environment built on comfort, community, and compassionate care, every day.

For inquiries, tours, or admissions questions, reach out to our administrator, Carolyne Rico — we welcome you to visit any day of the week and see the community for yourself.
