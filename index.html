Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the basic care package?

Our basic care package includes 24/7 supervision, nutritious meals, medication management, and social activities. We also offer personalized care plans based on individual needs.

Can we visit anytime?

We are open for tours daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Residents can receive visitors during operating hours. For special arrangements, please contact us directly.

Do you accept VA benefits?

Yes! We are proud to assist veterans and surviving spouses in utilizing VA Aid & Attendance benefits. Our team can help guide you through the application process.

What makes your hospice care special?

We are officially approved for 8 hospice beds, allowing residents to receive compassionate, dignified end-of-life care in the comfort of a familiar home environment with our caring team.

How do you handle emergency situations?

Our trained 24/7 staff is equipped to handle medical emergencies. We have protocols in place and work closely with local healthcare providers to ensure rapid response when needed.

Can residents bring personal belongings?

Yes, residents are encouraged to personalize their rooms with family photos, furniture, and other meaningful items to make their space feel like home.
